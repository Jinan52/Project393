----------------------------------------
Idea 1: Magic Digger
----------------------------------------

Description:
A digging-based game inspired by Digger, where the player explores underground tunnels and collects gold while avoiding enemies.

Unique Feature:
The game includes magical stones that grant special abilities:
- Speed boost
- Freezing enemies
- Double digging power

Gameplay:
The player moves through the map, digs tunnels, collects gold, and uses abilities strategically to survive and win.

Why it is interesting:
Combines classic gameplay with dynamic abilities that change strategy and player decisions.


----------------------------------------
Idea 2: Time Maze
----------------------------------------

Description:
A maze game where the player must escape enemies and reach the goal.

Unique Feature:
The player can rewind time for a few seconds to fix mistakes or escape dangerous situations.

Gameplay:
The player navigates a maze while avoiding enemies. If trapped, they can reverse time and try a different path.

Why it is interesting:
Introduces a time-based mechanic that adds depth and creativity to standard maze gameplay.


----------------------------------------
Idea 3: Eco Defender
----------------------------------------

Description:
A strategy-based game where the player cleans pollution from the environment.

Unique Feature:
The player must balance cleaning and defending areas from enemies that re-contaminate them.

Gameplay:
Collect resources, clean areas, and build defenses to protect clean zones.

Why it is interesting:
Adds a meaningful theme (environment) with strategic gameplay elements.
